Quality Tips (Q-Tips)

Overview
The QMO is the thin, common sense, standard methodology used for software development. The QMO specifies the "what", while the "how" is captured as part of the local procedures. The QMO is an electronic document. There are seven Life Cycle Phases, an Introduction, and an Appendix. See Quality Management System Overview for more detail.
